Simple bathroom remodel ideas can make your space look and feel new even if your budget doesn't allow for a head-to-toe transformation.

Basement Renovation

Basements can be daunting spaces for remodeling. Cluttered and dark, basements should convince you to turn your attention to other projects in the home. But they can be remodeled and finished so that they not only integrate with the rest of the home but become beautiful and valuable assets to the property.

Tiling

Many persons underestimate the importance of floors. However, we believe it is one of the most important elements in your living space. Sleek and stylish tiling will transform even the most dull spaces to with one of elegance.

Painting

The selection of appropriate colors for your space is needed to bring off any section of your home. Major Renovations and Tiling will transform your living space with immaculate painting to refreshed and brightened rooms.

Drywall

Drywall is a construction material used to create walls and ceilings. It's also used to create many design features, including eaves, arches and other architectural specialties. It's incredibly durable, and requires only simple repairs when damaged.
